What changes with regular practice

You start noticing the moment before you react — the half-second where you'd normally freeze, fight, or disappear. And in that half-second, you find something new is possible.

You build tolerance for being seen. You stop performing safety and start actually feeling it. You find out what your relationships could be if you weren't running the same loops.

This isn't insight work. It's practice. You do it with other people, in real time, and something in your body rewires.
